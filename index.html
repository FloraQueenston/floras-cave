About Flora

Flora is a vanilliafox, and probably the only one of his species left.

He likes to bite, be mean, scratch and growl. Oh and he steals socks for some reason.

Flora is 6'1f tall and has a very long and fluffy tail.

Oh and you should try to avoid touching the Tail, it's sensitive and he could get mad.

He prefers to eat raw meat and uncooked meals in general.

After years flora got found by someone who is now his owner.

He is taking good care of him and watches out that flora won't kill or hurt anyone.

Thats most of the stuff you need to know about Flora.
