=Additional information=

Vanilliafoxes have 2 different private parts.

So they have a Girl and Boy part between their legs.

This makes it possible for two vanniliafoxes with the same gender,

to make puppies.

You can see if a vanniliafox is a male or female on the tail and the ears.

Big Fluffy tail means it's a male.

Big Fluffy Ears means it's a female.
